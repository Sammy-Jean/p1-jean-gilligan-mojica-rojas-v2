About Us

Our History

Founded in 2009 by the Doulaveris family. Empire Pizza has grown into four locations stretching over both York & Lancaster County. We would like to thank our community for being a part of our journey over the last decade. We owe much of our success to your loyalty and support. We hope that you will continue on this journey with us!

-Doulaveris Family

Our Mission

At Empire Pizza we believe in tradition. We believe that the best food comes from the best ingredients. And the best ingredients make for a great slice of pizza. At Empire Pizza we value family and know that a meal together is time well spent. We've been serving great food and drinks for over ten years and want that tradition to become a part of yours. With a menu that has something for everyone, Empire Pizza continues to be the tradition. Empire Pizza. Eat. Drink. Gather.
